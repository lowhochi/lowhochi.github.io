platform   n(c)   //  

a long, flat raised structure at a railway station, where people get on and off trains

1/ The train drew into the platform.

1/ What platform does it go from?

1/ "Platform Nine and Three-Quarters is the train platform from which
  students board the Hogwarts Express, the scarlet steam engine that brings
  students to and from Hogwarts."
