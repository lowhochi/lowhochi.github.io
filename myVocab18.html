prank   n(c)   //

a trick that is played on somebody as a joke

1/ "What a childish prank"

1/ The incident was a prank that went wrong.

1/ I've had enough of your childish pranks.

play a prank on somebody

1/ "I played a prank on a classmate."

1/ When I was at school we were always playing pranks on our teachers.

1/ "It was a rainy day and I couldn't go out, so I decided to
  play a prank on my parents."

1/ She's always playing pranks on her little brother.

1/ "Steve frequently played pranks on others at school."

a prank call, a prank phone call

1/ At first I thought it was a prank call.

1/ "Once my cousin made a prank call to me. It was all so funny."

1/ They decided to have a little fun and make some prank calls.
