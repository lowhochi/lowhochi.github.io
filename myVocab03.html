hold back

1/ She held back from interfering in their arguments.

2/ Sandbags will hold back the flood waters for a while

  what I want to do in life.

  the school may advise holding them back.

8/ Does holding kids back a year help them academically?

  COVID-19 interruptions and differences in digital learning opportunities?

hold someone/something back: to stop someone/something developing
  or doing as well as they should

10/ She felt that having children would hold her back.

11/ She hadn't got the promotion she wanted, and she accused her boss of
